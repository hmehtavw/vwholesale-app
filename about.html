Who We Are

V Wholesale is a retail and wholesale home building store operated by Vassure Wholesale Pvt. Ltd., based in Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh. We serve homeowners, interior contractors, plumbers, electricians, painters, and builders across the Krishna and Guntur districts.

We stock tiles, sanitaryware, plumbing fittings, electricals, paints, hardware, tools, appliances, plywood, adhesives, grouts, and waterproofing solutions — all under one roof at our NH65, Bhavanipuram showroom.

Our Story

V Wholesale was founded to solve a real problem: getting quality home building materials in Tier 2 India means visiting multiple shops, negotiating separately, and hoping everything arrives on time. We built the store we wished existed.

Starting from our showroom in Bhavanipuram, Vijayawada, we serve walk-in retail customers, B2B buyers, and now online customers through this platform. Our Contractor Club brings the trade community together under one ecosystem.
