Delivery Coverage

We currently deliver within Vijayawada city limits, including Bhavanipuram, Governorpet, Labbipet, Suryaraopeta, Moghalrajpuram, Patamata, Gandhinagar, Ajit Singh Nagar, Gunadala, and surrounding areas.

For deliveries outside Vijayawada (Guntur, Krishna district), please contact us directly to discuss logistics and charges.

Delivery Timelines

  • Quick Delivery: 90-minute target for in-stock items within city limits. This is a target, not a guarantee — actual time may vary due to traffic or demand.
  • Scheduled Delivery: Same day (orders placed before 2 PM) or next business day.
  • Bulk / Project: Confirmed by our logistics team within 24 hours of order placement.
  • Special / Custom orders: Timeline communicated at the time of order confirmation.

Important Notes

  • Delivery timelines are estimates and may be affected by factors beyond our control (traffic, weather, high demand).
  • A confirmation SMS or WhatsApp will be sent when your order is dispatched.
  • Please ensure someone is available at the delivery address. If the delivery cannot be completed, we will attempt re-delivery once. A second failed delivery may incur an additional charge.
  • For heavy or fragile items (large tiles, bathtubs), please ensure ground-floor or ground-level access. Our team is not equipped for upper-floor deliveries without prior arrangement.
  • Inspect goods at delivery. Report any damage or discrepancy immediately to the delivery person and within 24 hours to our team.
